What do I need for class?

There is a short list of things that you will need to bring with you to your class.

1. Your Arkansas Driver's License or Arkansas ID Card.

2. Your handgun and at least 30 rounds of ammunition. (or you can borrow one of our handguns, we provide the ammunition)

3. Your eye and ear protection. (we have some for you to borrow, if you'd like)

4. A check for $141.25 made out to Arkansas State Police for your license fees and background check. (you can wait up to 6 months before you send your packet in to ASP)

5. Your class tutition.

6. Sunscreen and bug spray.
