About Us

Our lead instructor, Aaron Johnson, has nine years in law enforcement, and holds three instructor certificates from the Arkansas Commission on Law Enforcement Standards and Training, including firearms instructor. He is certified and approved by the Arkansas State Police as a Handgun Safety Instructor. His Instructor number is 06-488. He has carried his handgun with an Arkansas permit since 1996.

Our associate instructor, Joshua Evans, has three years with the Arkansas Department of Correction, holds several training certificates through ADC. He has carried his handgun with an Arkansas permit since 2007.

OzarksHandgun.com is owned, operated and copyrighted by Panther Public Safety, based in Mountain Home, Baxter County, Arkansas.
