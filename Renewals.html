License Renewals

 
   

We can help you renew your license.

Renewing your license is easy. Call us no more than 90 days before the expiration date on your license. We will meet with you at a firing range so you can demonstrate that you are still proficient with your handgun. We will discuss any changes pertaining your permit. We will help you fill out the renewal application, or you can fill it out on-line.

We charge $20 for a renewal, and Arkansas State Police charges $60. A late fee of $15 applies if State Police receive your renewal packet after the expiration date of your license.
